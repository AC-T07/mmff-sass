Become a Sponsor

There are plenty of great ways to get exposure for your organization by supporting our festival. Connect with community, discover new clients, and get your business seen and heard! As a not-for-profit organization, we rely on the generosity and kind-heartedness of our community to make the festival possible. Your contributions aid us in paying fees to artists, and help us make our next event a special and rewarding experience for filmmakers and audiences alike.

Tickets & Passes

Tickets

SINGLE TICKET ($10)

Grants admission to one event (a screening or performance). All sales final.

Single tickets can purchased online in advance. Online payments can be made through PayPal and most major credit cards:

Single tickets are also available at the box office anytime during the festival (cash only).

Passes

FESTIVAL PASS ($25)

Grants admission to every event in the 2-day festival. All sales final.

Festival passes can purchased online in advance. Online payments can be made through PayPal and most major credit cards:

Festival passes can also be purchased at the box office anytime during the festival (cash only).

Because of limited seating, pass holders must still pick up tickets to individual events from the box office. Ticket quantities are based on venue capacity and are offered on a first-come, first-serve basis - so arrive early!

All filmmakers who submit their work to Mirror Mountain automatically receive a festival pass.

Tickets & Passes

Tickets

SINGLE TICKET ($10)

Grants admission to 1 event only (a screening or performance).

Tickets can purchased online in advance. Payments can be made through PayPal and most major credit cards:

Tickets are also available on a pay-what-you-can basis at the LabO box office anytime from July 21-23 (cash only). All sales final.

Passes

WEEKEND PASS ($35)

Grants admission to every event in the 3-day festival.

Passes can purchased online in advance. Payments can be made through PayPal and most major credit cards:

Passes are also available at the LabO box office anytime from July 21-23 (cash only). All sales final.

Because of limited seating, pass holders must still get their pass stamped at the box office prior to each event in order to gain access to the theatre. Entrance will be granted based on venue capacity on a first-come, first-serve basis - so arrive early!

Doors open 15 minutes before each screening.
